Journo suit: Busy as a bee

Run! Run! Run! Run as fast as you can! If you can fly, do so.

5/7/2022 · 1 min read

When I was in junior high school, I was a part of our journalism club which its name was 'The Harbinger'. It was named as The Harbinger because just like birds, we, the journos, are required to be as fast as them when it comes to covering different events that were happening inside our campus at that time. We had to be swift, fast-thinkers and ready all the time.

My experience in journalism is one of my most unforgettable memories in junior high school. It brought me to different places with people of different personalities which opened me up to a variety of learnings in life.

I miss the moments when I was already in the contest proper and I was feeling the pressure in being surrounded by a lot of intelligent students from different schools. This is probably the thing I would like to experience again in my life.
