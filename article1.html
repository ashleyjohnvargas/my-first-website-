How I wish to be at MIT

It was a lie when they said MIT is not the standard.

5/7/2022 · 1 min read

Who would not dream to study at the most prestigious and highly in-demand university in the world right now? Massachusetts Institute of Technology is undoubtedly the dream school of every student dreaming to become a web developer someday. Its name, its faculty, its prowess and beauty are all that make it as a dream school for everyone.

A lot of successful and well-known web developers, front-end and back-end, came from MIT. This proves that the education and curriculum there is incomparable to other universities. The facilities, technology and the proctors themselves are excellent.

Unfortunately, MIT is just a wishful thinking for me. Its great excellence and reputation requires great amount of money. And as a simple Filipino from a family of low-income class, to study at MIT is a fantasy which I wish a reality.
